What we have from investor:

New ones: 
- Name
- Company (Firm)
- Typical check size (Min / Max check values)
- Bio
- Investment sector focus (Interests - presets + custom tags)
- Stage focus (Expanded: Pre-seed, Seed, Series A/B/C, Growth, Venture Debt, Angel, Grants, Non-dilutive)
- Trust score (Score & breakdown metrics)
- Technical choices (3-state preferences: escrow, NDA, open source, hardware, cryptography)
- Pace (Investments per quarter)
- Custom tags (Custom matching keywords)
- Notes (Investor Notes ledger)
- Lead status (Lead / Follow-only / Flexible)
- Allowed legal structures (Delaware C-Corp, Singapore PTE, Cayman Foundation, custom structures)
- Investment vehicles accepted (Direct Fund, SPV, Syndicate, Venture Debt)
- Operational support / superpowers (Recruitment, sales, regulatory, follow-on syndication)
- Co-investment networks (Frequent syndicate partner funds/angels)

Old ones:
- Startups he clicked on (View/Click history logs)
- Past top matches he disliked and liked (Feedback loops on primary matches)
- Past bonus matches he liked and disliked (Feedback loops on speculative/spec profiles)
- His search history (Historical query strings & applied filters)
- His investments (Active portfolio tracking, committed funds, released capital, milestone receipts)
